ACCESSIBILITY STATEMENT

Awaken Spa is committed to making our website accessible for all customers, including those with disabilities.

Consistent with this goal, we have taken a number of steps to enhance our site and increase its usability by those who access the web using assistive technology. Our efforts are guided by the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines, version 2.0, Level A (WCAG 2.0 AA). We will continue to enhance our digital properties to deliver accessible experiences.
